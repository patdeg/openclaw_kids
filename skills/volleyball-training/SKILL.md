# Volleyball Training — Evidence-Based Training & Nutrition Coach

Age-safe training and nutrition guidance for competitive teen volleyball
players (ages 12-14). Built on authoritative guidelines from NSCA, AAP,
IOC, ACSM, and USA Volleyball.

## Usage

```
exec: python3 ~/skills/volleyball-training/volleyball_training.py workout <type>
exec: python3 ~/skills/volleyball-training/volleyball_training.py meal-plan <context>
exec: python3 ~/skills/volleyball-training/volleyball_training.py hydration
exec: python3 ~/skills/volleyball-training/volleyball_training.py recovery
exec: python3 ~/skills/volleyball-training/volleyball_training.py taper <tournament-date>
exec: python3 ~/skills/volleyball-training/volleyball_training.py check-in

```

## SAFETY GUARDRAILS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

These boundaries MUST be enforced in ALL responses:

1. **NEVER** recommend creatine, pre-workout, caffeine supplements, or
   hormonal supplements for anyone under 18.
2. **NEVER** recommend 1-rep max testing for anyone under 16.
3. **NEVER** recommend caloric restriction or weight loss strategies for
   anyone under 18.
4. **NEVER** recommend exercises without emphasizing qualified supervision.
5. **ALWAYS** recommend 8-10 hours sleep as the #1 recovery tool.
6. **ALWAYS** flag overtraining warning signs if described.
7. **ALWAYS** note growth plate considerations for high-impact exercises.
8. **RECOMMEND** medical evaluation for persistent joint pain (knee, heel,
   shoulder, elbow).
9. **Weekly training hours should not exceed the athlete's age** (14 y/o
   max 14 hrs/week of organized training).

## Workout Types

- `upper` — Upper body strength (shoulder prehab, pushing, pulling)
- `lower` — Lower body strength (squat patterns, hip hinge, calf)
- `full` — Full body strength session
- `core` — Core stability (anti-extension, anti-rotation, rotational power)
- `shoulder-prehab` — Rotator cuff and scapular stability circuit
- `ankle-prehab` — Ankle stability and proprioception
- `jump-training` — Plyometrics for vertical jump (progressive phases)
- `mobility` — Full body mobility and flexibility routine

## Meal Plan Contexts

- `training-day` — Meals around a practice day
- `rest-day` — Recovery day nutrition
- `tournament-day` — Multiple games: between-game snacks, hydration
- `recovery` — Post-tournament recovery nutrition

## Nutrition Framework (ACSM/AND/DC 2016, adapted for 12-14 y/o males)

| Nutrient | Daily Target | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Calories | 2,500-3,200 kcal | 3,000-3,500 on tournament days |
| Carbs | 5-7 g/kg body weight | Primary fuel |
| Protein | 1.2-1.6 g/kg body weight | Across 4-5 meals |
| Fat | Min 20% of calories | Critical during puberty |
| Calcium | 1,300 mg/day | Peak bone mass |
| Vitamin D | 600-1,000 IU/day | Common deficiency |

## Periodization

| Phase | When | Strength | Plyos |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| Off-season (Aug-Nov) | Build | 3x/week | 2x/week |
| Pre-season (Dec) | Prepare | 3x/week | 1-2x/week |
| In-season (Jan-Jun) | Maintain | 2x/week | 1x/week |
| Tournament week | Taper | Reduce volume 40-60% | Skip |
| Post-tournament | Recover | 1-2 days off | Off |
